# SmartRower Pro training plans

## Foundation - Learn the Stroke (4 weeks, difficulty 1)

Four weeks, three short sessions a week, to build the stroke before the engine: Beta 'technique' ghost, catch and sequence drills, easy aerobic volume. Graduate when the Coach tab stays quiet for ten strokes in a row.

**Week 1**: Beta Shape 20min @18spm - Regeneration 20min - Connection Row 16min

**Week 2**: Beta Shape 25min @18spm - Sequence Drill 1 - Recovery Flow 1

**Week 3**: Catch Sharpener 6x3min - Beta Shape 20min @20spm - UT2 Steady 30min

**Week 4**: Pause Drill Finish 16min - Beta Shape 25min @20spm - Regeneration 25min

## Aerobic Engine (6 weeks, difficulty 2)

Six weeks of progressive aerobic volume periodised on TRIMP: steady UT2/UT1, sweet spot and distance monuments, with the Pw:HR decoupling metric as the weekly pass/fail. Four sessions a week.

**Week 1**: UT2 Steady 40min - Sweet Spot 20min - Regeneration 20min - Distance Row 5k

**Week 2**: UT2 Steady 50min - Aerobic Waves 3x10min - Recovery Flow 2 - Distance Row 6k

**Week 3**: UT2+ Steady 40min - Sweet Spot 30min - Regeneration 25min - Negative Split 40min

**Week 4**: UT2 Steady 60min - Aerobic Waves 4x10min - Recovery Flow 1 - Distance Row 8k

**Week 5**: UT1 Steady 40min - Sweet Spot 40min - Regeneration 30min - Negative Split 50min

**Week 6**: Distance Row 10k - UT1 Steady 30min - Recovery Flow 3 - UT2 Steady 40min

## 2k Breakthrough - SmartRower Edition (6 weeks, difficulty 4)

Six weeks from benchmark to race: threshold and W'-engineered intervals whose depletion floors are computed with the Skiba model, race pieces on the 'race' ghost, and a proper taper into the 2k simulation. Four sessions a week.

**Week 1**: 5k Tempo Test - Threshold 4x10min - Regeneration 25min - UT2 Steady 40min

**Week 2**: Over-Under 4x(2+3) 108/92 - W' Burn 8x60s @130% - Recovery Flow 2 - UT2 Steady 40min

**Week 3**: Threshold 3x15min - HIIT 10x40/20s - Regeneration 25min - Race Pieces 8x250m

**Week 4**: Over-Under 5x(2+3) 105/90 - W' Burn 6x90s @125% - Recovery Flow 1 - Race Pieces 4x500m

**Week 5**: 1k Time Trial - Depletion Blocks 3x4min - Regeneration 20min - 2k Split Strategy: Fast Finish

**Week 6**: Rate Surges 4x(3+1) - Start Sequence Practice - Regeneration 15min - 2k Race Simulation

## Time-Crunched 30 (4 weeks, difficulty 3)

Four weeks, three sessions of about 30 minutes: sweet spot for the engine, HIIT with computed W' floors for the top end, recovery to absorb it. Maximum adaptation per minute for busy weeks.

**Week 1**: Sweet Spot 20min - HIIT 10x30/30s - Recovery Flow 1

**Week 2**: Cube Ladder 16-22spm x4 - HIIT 16x20/10s - Regeneration 20min

**Week 3**: Sweet Spot 30min - Sprint Repeats 6x15s - Recovery Flow 2

**Week 4**: Threshold 6x6min - Tabata Classic - Regeneration 20min
